About Me

I'm a data scientist and researcher working at Fundar. ​I'm passionate about leveraging data to drive meaningful public policy design and contribute to projects with a lasting social impact. Currently, my lines of research focus on quantitative applications of productive and sectoral policies and data governance in the state.​

I've also been a teacher for over eight years. Nowadays I'm a Professor of Fundamentals of Computational Social Science at Universidad Nacional Guillermo Brown and a Teaching Assistant of Macroeconomics at Universidad de Buenos Aires.​
